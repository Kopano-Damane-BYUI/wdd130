White water rafting

White water rafting on the Zambezi River is an adrenaline-fueled adventure like no other, offering an exhilarating journey through some of the world’s most spectacular rapids. As the river carves its way through the deep gorges of the Batoka Gorge below Victoria Falls, thrill-seekers are treated to a heart-pounding ride through class III to V rapids, including infamous stretches like “The Devil’s Toilet Bowl” and “Oblivion.” Discover the ultimate adventure with Wild Horizons packages and specials.
